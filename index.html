Fielderr

Fielderr is a field service system software. It is a territory mapping tool with tasks dispatching and tracking features. Fielderr is a B2B software that manages day to day operations of field working businesses such as pharma companies in which workers visit pharmacies to ensure their operations run smoothly. This tool manages pharmacies locations and sales representatives' visits to pharmacies and tracking of tasks assigned to them. Other features include users management dashboard, products management, task dispatching and submission of completed tasks, location sharing, tasks automation and a lot more.
